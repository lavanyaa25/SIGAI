What is ACM SIGAI?


"ACM SIGAI, The Special Interest Group for Artificial Intelligence was founded in New York in 1966, is the world’s largest educational and scientific society connecting and convening the researchers, educators, and professionals to apportion resources, inspire discourse and scrutinize and address the field’s challenges. ACM’s reach extends to every part of the globe, with more than half of its 100,000 members residing outside the U.S.

Manipal University Jaipur ACM SIGAI Chapter was officially chartered on 11th November 2022 in esteemed presence of Dr.Madhavan Mukund and Mrs. Hemangee Kapoor. MUJ ACM SIGAI offers series of Competitions, Workshops and Events. Our Members are also offered Mentorship in the form of ROAD TO AI from their seniors who are pursuing a career in the specific domain.
